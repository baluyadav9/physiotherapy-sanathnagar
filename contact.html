Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need a doctor's referral? +

No referral needed — you can book directly. However, if you have X-rays, MRI scans, or medical reports, please bring them as they help us plan your treatment.

How long is a typical session? +

Initial assessment sessions are 45–60 minutes. Follow-up treatment sessions are 30–45 minutes. Duration varies based on your condition and treatment required.

Do you offer home visits? +

Yes — home visits are available for post-surgery, elderly, or bedridden patients in Hyderabad. Call us to discuss availability and charges.

How many sessions will I need? +

It depends on your condition and your body's response. After an initial assessment, our physiotherapist will give you a clear treatment plan with an estimated number of sessions.
