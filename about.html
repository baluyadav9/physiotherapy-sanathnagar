Who We Are

Built on a Foundation of Care & Expertise

Physiotherapy Sanathnagar was founded with a vision to bring world-class rehabilitation care to the people of Sanathnagar and surrounding areas of Hyderabad.

What started as a small clinic has grown into a comprehensive physiotherapy centre with a team of specialist physiotherapists, modern equipment, and a reputation built entirely on patient outcomes and trust.

Over the years, we've helped thousands of patients recover from injuries, surgeries, and chronic pain. We're proud to serve patients from Sanathnagar, KPHB, Kukatpally, Ameerpet, Miyapur, and across Hyderabad.
