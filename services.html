What We Offer

Our Physiotherapy Services

Comprehensive, evidence-based care for every stage of your recovery journey.

🏃

Sports Injury Rehabilitation

Whether you're a weekend warrior or a competitive athlete, our sports physio team uses advanced manual therapy, strength training, and functional movement analysis to get you back to peak performance. We treat ligament tears, rotator cuff injuries, muscle strains, shin splints, and more.

ACL / PCL Injury Rotator Cuff Ankle Sprains Tennis Elbow Muscle Strains
🔬

Post-Surgery Rehabilitation

Surgery is only half the journey — recovery is where we come in. Our structured post-operative rehab programmes are designed in collaboration with your surgeon to restore strength, flexibility, and full function after knee/hip replacement, ACL reconstruction, spinal surgery, and more.

Knee Replacement Hip Replacement Spinal Surgery ACL Reconstruction Shoulder Surgery
🦴

Spine & Back Pain Care

Our spine specialists use a combination of manual therapy, targeted exercises, postural correction, and ergonomic advice to address the root cause of your pain. We treat acute back strains, chronic cervical spondylosis, sciatica, herniated discs, and more.

Lower Back Pain Cervical Spondylosis Sciatica Herniated Disc Postural Correction
🧠

Neurological Rehabilitation

Our neuro rehab team works with stroke survivors, Parkinson's patients, and those with spinal cord injuries to improve balance, coordination, gait, and independence. We use neuroplasticity-based techniques to help the brain and body re-learn movement patterns.

Stroke Recovery Parkinson's Disease Multiple Sclerosis Spinal Cord Injury Balance Disorders
💆

Chronic Pain Management

We offer a multidisciplinary approach to chronic pain including TENS therapy, ultrasound therapy, dry needling, manual therapy, and progressive exercise rehabilitation. We treat fibromyalgia, rheumatoid arthritis, chronic knee/shoulder pain, and more.

TENS Therapy Dry Needling Arthritis Fibromyalgia Ultrasound Therapy
🤝

Paediatric Physiotherapy

Our paediatric physiotherapists create fun, engaging, and effective therapy sessions for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, muscular dystrophy, flat feet, scoliosis, and post-injury recovery. We work closely with parents to continue progress at home.

Cerebral Palsy Developmental Delays Muscular Dystrophy Flat Feet / Scoliosis
👴

Geriatric Physiotherapy

Our geriatric physio programme focuses on fall prevention, improving balance and strength, managing age-related joint pain, and maintaining independence. We work gently and effectively with elderly patients to improve quality of life and keep them active.

Fall Prevention Balance Training Osteoporosis Osteoarthritis
🌸

Posture Correction & Women's Health

We offer evidence-based treatment for postural disorders like lumbar lordosis, forward head posture, and scoliosis, along with women's health physiotherapy including prenatal/postnatal rehab and pelvic floor dysfunction treatment.

Posture Correction Prenatal Physio Postnatal Recovery Pelvic Floor
